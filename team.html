Good Hood Club
Chloe, Eloise

The Good Hood Club is a loungewear company dedicated to championing pediatric oncology, most notably through donating 50% of our profits to the Pediatric Oncology Group of Ontario (POGO).

The purpose is in the name, "Good Hood Club" represents the mission of creating comfort for those in the community going through their own unique battle and reminding all that you are "stronger than you think".
